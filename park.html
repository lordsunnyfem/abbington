The Play Park


Abbington Farm Play park has a breath-taking selection of outdoor and adventurous activities.


With activities designed to meet the needs of all ages and experiences of young people, Abbington Farm truly brings adventure to everyone.


Each activity is run under the instruction of one of our highly trained staff and all safety equipment is provided.


Some of these activities are hayrides, pumpkin picking, and a corn maze.


Our activities are very popular with youth groups including Scouts and Guides.


You can also:


- visit the animals and see how they live in their natural habitat


- play with farm tools, such as a pitch fork or shovel


- pick your own produce from the garden to take home with you!


Our centre also provides ideal settings and opportunities for school groups to offer exciting recreational activities and skills acquisition experiences or training to students.




Kits, footwear and clothings


Appropriate kits, footwear and clothings for activities, if required, could be purchased from sports shops nationwide such as Decathlon and JD Sports, or through their websites.


We are open all week - weekdays and weekends, so we can offer you a choice of times tailored to fit around your schedule.
