Our Farm Shop


Welcome to our farm shop website, where you can find all the information that you need about our products.


The farm shop has a wide range of fresh produce and products, from jams and preserves to eggs and bacon.


We have free-range eggs that are laid by the chickens on our farm.


Our pork is reared in the fields around our house, giving it a distinctive flavour.


Choose from award winning pies with fillings such as: apricot or blackberry; hand-made chocolates; local honey and marmalade; homemade breads and cakes; locally sourced fruit juices, wines, beers and ciders.


We also sell fresh vegetables grown in our garden, as well as milk, yogurt, cheese and fresh fruits!




We are open all week - weekdays and weekends, so we can offer you a choice of times tailored to fit around your schedule.
